THREE REEFS is a privately held financial group incorporated in the Cayman Islands. The group has two operating companies: THREE REEFS TRADING SEZC and THREE REEFS GLOBAL.

THREE REEFS TRADING SEZC is a proprietary trading entity and single-family office. It trades commodities and financial instruments across global markets. It does not solicit or accept outside capital. Three Reefs Trading SEZC is registered as an Exempted Special Economic Zone Company (No. SN-433268).

THREE REEFS GLOBAL provides specialized solutions and services to Three Reefs Trading SEZC, qualified institutional financial entities, and professional clients. It does not serve retail or individual customers. Three Reefs Global is incorporated as an Exempted Company (No. SN-433266).
