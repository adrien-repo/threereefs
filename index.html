The Group

Three Reefs is structured around two core units:
Trading and Global.

Three Reefs is a privately held financial group incorporated in the Cayman Islands. The group has two operating companies: Three Reefs Trading SEZC and Three Reefs Global.

Both entities operate from George Town, Grand Cayman. Three Reefs is a member of the Cayman Commodities and Derivatives Centre.

Three Reefs Trading SEZC

Proprietary Trading
& Single-Family Office

Exempted Special Economic Zone Company (No. SN-433268)

Three Reefs Trading SEZC is a proprietary trading entity and single-family office. It trades commodities and financial instruments across global markets. It does not solicit or accept outside capital.


The entity manages a concentrated portfolio of high-conviction ideas across commodities, equities, derivatives, futures and options, with a focus on analytical rigor, structural inefficiencies, special situations and asymmetric opportunities.

Three Reefs Global

Solutions & Services

Exempted Company (No. SN-433266)

Three Reefs Global provides specialized solutions and services to Three Reefs Trading SEZC, qualified institutional financial entities, and professional clients operating across global markets.


Engagements involve complex mandates across multiple jurisdictions and asset classes, with a high degree of technical expertise and professional discretion.


Three Reefs Global does not engage with, or offer its services to, retail or individual customers.
